# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Internship
Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship

## Task
Task 1 – Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-performing categories, and regional sales performance using Power BI.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- CSV Dataset
- GitHub

## Dashboard Features
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Sales by Region
- Top 10 Products
- Interactive Slicers

## Key Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales.
- East region achieved the highest revenue.
- Sales increased during peak months.
- Furniture had lower profitability.

## Recommendations
- Increase focus on Technology products.
- Improve Furniture profit margins.
- Expand marketing in high-performing regions.
- Use seasonal promotions to boost sales.
